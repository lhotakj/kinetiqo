Track your Fitness (CTL), Fatigue (ATL), and Form (TSB) over time.

About these metrics

  • Fitness (CTL): Chronic Training Load. A 42-day weighted average of your daily stress. Represents long-term training volume.
  • Fatigue (ATL): Acute Training Load. A 7-day weighted average. Represents recent tiredness.
  • Form (TSB): Training Stress Balance (Fitness - Fatigue). Positive means fresh, negative means fatigued.

Based on the impulse-response model by Banister et al. (1975). Read more at TrainingPeaks.
